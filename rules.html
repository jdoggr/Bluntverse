Server Rules

Current server rules (rules are subject to change, it is your job to keep updated on them):

  • All conversations must be in English only, due to mods and other members not being able to understand other languages and it is too much work on staff to have to translate everything. There will be a global chat if you'd like to speak another language.
  • Spamming will not be allowed in the main chat due to flooding and people will not be able to keep up and conversations may slip by.
  • Keep it simple and clean, reminder we have members that are like 13 and we promise they do not want to see that stuff, make NSFW jokes in the adult channels, if staff see you doing this you will be punished fairly.
  • Do not drop any kind of links or tell members to join other servers without mod approval, if you do it without mod approval you will be punished.
  • No hate speech, slurs, harassment, or anything, be respectful to staff and members at all times.
  • You must be 13+ to join if we find out you are under this age you will be banned instantly and may join back once you reach age 13 and can prove you are 13.
  • No doxxing or sharing your own or others personal information, we want everyone to be safe.
  • If a mod tells you to do something, you listen instantly, if you have issues with something a mod did or said then please create a ticket and complain, mods are alo here to keep the peace going.
  • Use the proper channels for their correct uses, do not derail discussions, when discusing political or religious topics please move to the debate channel.
  • Do not pretend to be another member, this includes staff, members, or anyone else, if discovered you will be punished.
  • No flirting with minors, no unsolicited DMs, no weird vibes, we want everyone to be comfortable in the server and being weird can make other users uncomfortable.
  • This server is not the place to bring chaos to, if you have issues open a ticket or move to dms with the user you have issues with, if you open a ticket staff may be able to assist with defusing the situation.
  • Slurs are not allowed for any reason, no bypassing or anything, this is because it can offend people and it can bring unwanted arguments and we do not want that, so please refrain from using slurs or you will be banned.
  • If any rules are not on here, staff still hold the right to punish you accordingly, if you were wrongly punished make a ticket to discuss with proof.
